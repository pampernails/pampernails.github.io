Welcome

Welcome to Pamper Nails — a luxury full-service nail salon in Royse City, Texas offering manicures, pedicures, waxing, and spa services. Every visit is paired with a complimentary drink from our menu of margaritas, mimosas, wine, beer, and more — because being pampered is the whole point.
